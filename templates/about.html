Chatbot of Truth
Understanding what are good questions to ask large language models

Chatbot of Truth is an interactive webpage inspired by the famous tourist spot Mouth of Truth in Rome, Italy, with the Chatbot (a modified ChatGPT model) as its backbone.
You can ask a yes-or-no question, and the you will get a yes-or-no answer based on chatbot's best knowledge, and whether it's a proper question to ask a chatbot. For instance, if you ask “Did I have coffee today”, it will answer....

  • Interaction: Navigate to "Interaction" page to ask boolean questions
  • Instruction: Navigate to "instruction" page for instructions
