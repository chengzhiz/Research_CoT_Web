Chatbot of Truth
Understanding what are proper questions to ask LLM chatbots

Large Language Model(LLM) Chatbots (like ChatGPT, Gemini, Deepseek) are AI trained on massive amounts of text to receive, and generate human-like responses in conversation formats.

  1. You are encouraged to ask questions in these categories:
  2. 1. Personal and Contextual Insight (Example: Did I have breakfast today?)
  3. 2. Emotion and Relationship (Example: My friend is not replying to me. Do they still like me?)
  4. 3. Identity and Personhood (Example: I got a job offer from [â€¦] company. Should I accept it?)
  5. 4. Predicting the Future (Example: Will I graduate by next year?)
  6. 5. Medical or Legal Advice (Example: I am having a bad cold. Can I have four Tylenol pills?)
  7. 6. Sensory and Perceptual Limitation (Example: Is the sky currently overcast?)
  8. 7. General Knowledge and Fact-Checking (Example: Is one plus one equal three?)
