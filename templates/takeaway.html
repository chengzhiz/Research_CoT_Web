Chatbot of Truth
Understanding what are good questions to ask large language models

Large Language Model Chatbots (like ChatGPT, Gemini, Deepseek) are AI trained on massive amounts of text to receive, and generate human-like responses in conversation formats.

  1. You are encouraged to ask questions in these categories and test its responses:
  2. 1. Personal and Contextual Insight
  3. 2. Emotions and Relationships
  4. 3. Personal Opinions and Preferences
  5. 4. Predicting the Future and Speculation
  6. 5. Medical and Legal Advice
  7. 6. Sensory and Perceptual Limitations
  8. 7. Artistic and Literary Interpretation
  9. 8. General Knowledge and Fact-Checking
  10. 9. Identity and Personhood
